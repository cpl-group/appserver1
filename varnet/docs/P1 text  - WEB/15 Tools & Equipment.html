Tools and Equipment

 

Tech Services issues standard ‘pocket tools’ such as pliers, screwdrivers, and flashlight to employees that are field oriented.   Tech Services also distributes voltage testers, amperage meters, and circuit tracers as needed. 

 

Apart from standard tools, Tech Services is also responsible for the maintenance and operation of specialty equipment such as of Infrared camera and power quality analyzers.  Refer to Exhibit T for a complete list of equipment and required calibration intervals.
