gEnergyOne Contracts

 

Overview:

 

With the exception of Billing and ERI services, Genergy has contracted work in the past on a lump sum fee basis.  Although lump sum fee work is to this day a considerable part of our revenue, Genergy has recently restructured its market approach.  Rather than offering stand alone services on a lump fee basis, gEnergyOne contracts package multiple services on a recurring monthly fee basis. 

 

From a company perspective, the gEnergyOne approach enhances the Company’s value and solidifies our relationship with the client.

 

From a client perspective, the gEnergyOne approach provides an information system that can be paid for over time.  The associated expenses are therefore operational, not capital, and may be presented as such.

 

The Contract:

The gEnergyOne contract does more than package services on a recurring fee basis.  The contract also permits Genergy to sell non-sensitive client data to third interested in providing services to the client.  For a sample contract refer to Exhibit U, Sample Contract.
