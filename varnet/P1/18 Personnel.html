Personnel

 

 

Overview:

Although ach employee or group has different core tasks, the company operates as one entity.  Below is a list of task performed by employees our group of employees.  It is important to stress that the job descriptions below have latitude and employees should not confine themselves within these boundaries.  It is important for each employee to understand the work being performed by others, to take initiative and to interact with others in a positive manner for the fortitude of all.
