Introduction

 

Introduction and Purpose of Document:

General Energy Services (Genergy) presents this document titled ‘Operations Manual’ as the authoritative document on company policy, structure and standard operating procedures.  This document was first introduced in the fall of 2001 and was later issued as revision ‘00’ in January of 2002.  Any future revisions will be issued as such at management’s discretion. 

 

Contents

This document is made up of two parts; Part One, Text, and Part Two, Exhibits.  It contains information on subjects ranging from standard office etiquette to new client set up, from recycling expectations to detailed departmental functionality and responsibilities.  In the pages to follow, our policies are defined along with aspect of our operation.  

 

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a positive work environment and a well trained work force.  Our intent is to service the Building and Facility Management industry by providing premiere engineering, technical and energy management services backed by state of the art software and information systems.  Our goal is to revolutionize the Energy industry by providing real time data on energy use and up to date information on energy distribution and expense recovery.

 

Employee Responsibilities

It is the duty of every employee to carefully read and familiarize himself or herself with the policies, methods and procedures outlined herein.  In addition, each employee must carefully read and accept the terms and conditions outlined in Section VI, Company Details, by signing the acknowledgment form at the end of that Section.
