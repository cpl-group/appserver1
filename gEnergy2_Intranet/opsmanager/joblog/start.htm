Job Log Quick Help
 
 Opening a Job show/hide help

To open a job, enter the job number in the pink field next to the "Open Job" label and click the OPEN button. You can do a quick search of the job log by clicking on the "Quick job search" link to the right of the OPEN button. A window will open where you can type a text to search for. The window will then search the job descriptions for your entered text.

To do a more advance search on the job log, click the "Go To Advance Search" link in the upper right hand corner of the job log window. From the Advance Search area, you can search for a text string in most fields of a job - project manager, customer, job description, etc. You can also limit your seach by selecting from the drop downs for Company and Job Type as well by selecting the job status and even exclude types of jobs by selecting from the Exclude drop down. Once you've made your selections. Click the SEARCH button to begin your search. Be patient. The search conducted is extensive and may take a few seconds to complete.

Once completed, you will see a list of found jobs below the search area. Click on any interested job line to view the job details. At the bottom of the page, you'll also find a breakdown of the types of jobs found. The corresponding color coding found next to each job line.
 Creating a Job show/hide help

To create a new job, click the "Create a New Job" link at the top of the job log window. This will open a new window. You will first then select the company you wish to create the job for, then you can enter the remaining elements of your job such as Job Description, Job Type, Customer, etc. There are some small differences in the new job area when opening jobs for different companies:

As of 10/26/2004:


- When selecting a Reading & Billing job for Genergy, you will have to also select the building the Reading & Billing job is for.
- Genergy Jobs - when you select a customer - the customers address information will automatically populate the address information for the job.
- Genergy Jobs are automatically imported into the Accounting system when opened in the job log. GESCO jobs have to be made available to the Accounting system after the job is opened by selecting the "Ready for Accounting Transfer" flag in the edit window for a job.

Save the job and it will close the window and load your new job in the main job log window.

QUICK TIP: SHOULD SOMETHING HAPPEN AFTER YOU HIT SAVE THAT PREVENTS THE JOB LOG WINDOW FROM REFRESHING WITH YOUR NEWLY CREATED JOB, FIRST DO A SEARCH FOR YOUR JOB BEFORE ATTEMPTING TO CREATE AGAIN.
 Manage Customers & Contacts show/hide help

To manage customers and contacts, click the "Manage Customers and Contacts" link at the top of the job window. This will open in the main job log window the Manage Customers and Contacts module. Select the company that the customer is in and the screen will reload with the list of customers entered under that company. Click on a customer line to open the customer in a new window. From here you can view or update the customer record or edit contacts under that customer. To edit the customer information, in the customer window that opens up, click the EDIT CUSTOMER button. To edit contact information, in the customer window that opens up, scroll to the contact you wish to edit and click the EDIT button located under the customer.

To Add a new customer, click the NEW CUSTOMER button in the Manage Customers and Contacts area in the main job log window. To add contacts, click the NEW CONTACT button in the Manage Customers and Contacts area in the main job log window. New contacts may be entered for existing customers only.
 Manage Vendors show/hide help

To manage vendors, click the "Manage Vendors" link at the top of the job window. This will open in the main job log window the Manage Vendors module. Select the company that the vendor is in and the screen will reload with the list of vendors entered under that company. Click on a vendor line to open the vendor in a new window. From here you can view or update the vendor record.
