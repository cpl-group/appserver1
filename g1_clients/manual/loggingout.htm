Logging of gEnergyOne

 

Logging off the gEnergyOne system is important to maintain a secure service.We ask that every user please logout at the end of a session.

To logout, click the logoff button at the buttom of the gEnergyOne Navigation window

  

note: some browsers do not support the total logout routine. at times, the log off button will not close your application window. feel free to close the window after clicking the log off button
