Examples
•Example: A tenant is requesting 10W/sq.ft. to be utilized for lighting and power circuits for an area of 10,000 Square feet (Rentable Vs. Usable).  How many watts and how many amps those this translate to?
•
• 10 W/Sq.Ft. x 10,000 Sq.Ft. = 100,000 Watts or 100KW
•
•In a building with a 208 Volt 3 phase 4 wire distribution system:
• Amps = 100KW ÷ (208V x 1.732 x .85PF) = 326.5 Amps
•
•In a building with a 480 Volt 3 phase 4 wire distribution system:
• Amps = 100KW ÷ (480V x 1.732 x .85PF) = 141.5 Amps
