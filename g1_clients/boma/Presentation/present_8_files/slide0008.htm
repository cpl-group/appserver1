Electric Current Formula
for a 3 phase 4 wire distribution system:
Electric Current  = Power ÷ (Volts x 1.732 x PF) = Amps
Example: A tenant has expressed that she needs to power a new UPS unit rated for 20 KW.  The building has a 208 Volt distribution system.  How many amps does this translate to?
Electric Current = 20KW ÷ (208 Volts x 1.732 x .85) = 65.3 Amps.
