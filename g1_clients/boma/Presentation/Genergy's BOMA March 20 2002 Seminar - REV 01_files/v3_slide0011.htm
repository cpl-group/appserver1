Basic Formulas
To Calculate Watts per Square Foot:
True Power ÷ Square Feet = W/Sq.Ft.
Example: A prospective tenant’s engineer has told you that the projected power
requirements for the space are of approximately 60KW.  The space in question is
about 9,600 square feet.
   60,000 Watts ÷ 9600 Square Feet = 6.25 Watts / Sq. Ft.
