SECTION 01300

 

SUBMITTALS

 

PART 1.00 – GENERAL

 

1.01.        SUMMARY

 

a.       Prior to the commencement of any work, the Contractor, along with proposed shut down dates, will be required to submit a construction schedule detailing when (dates) the work will be 50%, 75% and 100% complete.

 

1.02.        SUBMITTAL PROCEDURES

 

A.       Coordinate submittals with performance of construction always allowing 5 working days for Landlord’s review and the review of Landlord’s Consultant. All submittals shall be formatted alike, submitted simultaneously to both parties and contain the following information:

a.   Project name

b.  Submittal number and title

c.   Date

d.  Instructions for all parties

e.   Name and address of the Contractor.

 

1.03.        SHOP DRAWINGS

 

A.       Contractor is to make available shop drawings upon request to either the Landlord or Landlord’s Consultant.   These shop drawings will show As Built conditions.

 

1.04.        PRODUCT DATA

  

A.       Collect Product Data from manufactures and issue, prior to work commencement, a single submittal including printed information, manufacturer’s installation/testing instructions, catalog cuts, dimension diagrams, standard wiring diagrams, and any other applicable information.
