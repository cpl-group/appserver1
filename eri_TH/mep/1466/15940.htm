1.5          CONDENSER WATER EQUIPMENT SERVICES

 

A.      Cooling Tower:  Energize fans when condenser water pump is started.

 

B.      Condenser Water:  Maintain condenser water supply temperature by modulating tower bypass valve and cooling tower fan speed.

 

1.       Operate bypass valve to maintain 65 deg F sump temperature.

2.       Operate cooling tower fans to maintain 70 deg F sump temperature.

3.       Close valve when unoccupied.

 

C.      Operator Station Display:  Indicate the following on operator workstation display terminal:

 

1.       Condenser water pump on-off indication (primary & secondary pumps).

2.       Low-level cooling tower sump alarm.

3.       Cooling tower fan on-off indication.

4.       Cooling tower sump heater on-off indication.

5.              Cooling tower dump indication.

6.              Cooling tower fan speed indication

7.              Bypass valve status

8.              Condenser water outlet temperature

9.              Fresh air fan speed indication
