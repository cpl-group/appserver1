About Us

 

 

Genergy performs real-time, web-enabled energy management services, specifically geared to owners of commercial, institutional, large residential and industrial properties.

GenergyTM was formed through the merger of two existing related companies, Consultants for Efficient Buildings, LLC (CEB) and Advance metering Systems, LLC (Advance). CEB has been providing electrical consulting and Engineering services primarily to commercial real estate owners. Advance has been providing energy services aimed at quantifying energy use and expenditures. The union of these two entities, with the addition of Genergys expertise in the Internet and web related field has positioned the Company well to provide powerful solutions to its customers and to target entirely new categories of customers. Genergy is already a leading integrated Metering Data Service Provider (MDSP) offering metering and billing services and Energy management consulting and services to multi-tenant commercial building owners in the New York City metro market.

Power Grid Identification (PGI)

PGI is a complete up to date on-line diagram that provides user-friendly computer generated schematics of a facility's entire electrical distribution system and related electrical power availability information.  It comes with 2 sets of color prints and includes all related charts such as riser schedules, sub-metering schedules and switchgear details.

Features of our Power Grid Identification Service include:

                     Floor by floor analysis of power distribution

                     Series of easily interpreted designs and color highlighted charts

                     Differentiates between base building and tenant power

                     Identifies and labels the number and location of electrical closets, riser shafts, distribution sources

                     Identifies forgotten or dormant risers

                     Diagrams high/low voltage lines, panels, metering equipment, disconnect switches and load centers

                     Generates power availability charts with detail square footage, riser's primary distribution, risers amperage capacity and watt per square foot availability

Benefits to the customer:

                     Assists in leasing activities

                     Helps Identify spare power  

                     Uncovers unsafe conditions and waste irregularities

                     Helps you run your facility efficiently and safely

Electric Rent Inclusion Service (ERI)

Many leases provide for billing tenant electricity on ERI basis, which include the following steps:

 

  • Complete electrical survey of tenants spaces, which indicates all power consuming equipment such as light, outlets, computers, copy machines, etc.

 

  • Calculating KWH and KW for these spaces.

 

  • Applying proper electrical rates to the consumption calculations.

 

Features of Electrical Rent Inclusion Service include:

 

  • Cursory review of rent inclusion clauses for each and every Tenant in the Building (performed once).

 

  • Initial setup of Tenant information onto ERI database (performed once).

 

  • Field Survey of all tenant spaces (performed twice, once following execution of the agreement and once at the 30th month anniversary of the agreement. Example: if given building has 500,000 square feet of space leased to tenants paying electricity on rent inclusion basis, Genergy will complete an initial field survey of the 500,000 square feet within the first 30 months, and then re-survey all 500,000 square feet on a first in first out basis after the 30th month anniversary of the agreement).

 

  • Field Survey Data Entry (performed twice, once following execution of the agreement and once at the 30th month anniversary of the agreement).

 

  • Energy Charge Calculations and Reports (once the above steps are completed for a given tenant, calculation of energy charges and related reports are performed on a quarterly basis to account for energy rate increases and other energy prices adjustments, commonly referred to as Monthly Adjustment Charges or MAC adjustments).

 

Our services not only improves upon building management control but also provides:

 

  • Increased profits and cash flow
  • Rapid return on investment
  • Increased net operating income and property values
  • Improved energy conservation
  • Control over energy waste
  • Protection against unbudgeted electricity expenses
  • Recovery of expenditures resulting from Utility driven Rate and Monthly Adjustment fluctuations inheret in our deregulated market

Infrared Radiometer Inspection (IRI)

As facility managers have come to realize, thermal scanning has become an important tool for preventative maintenance.  For over ten years, GENERGY has been performing IRI's to visualize and record important and potentially hazardous thermal events.  Infrared Radiometer Inspection, commonly known as thermal imaging reporting, is used to locate hot spots and surface temperature differentials, which if left undetected could result in costly and hazardous breakdowns.

Our Infrared Radiometer Inspection includes:

         Thermal picture (thermo gram)

         Identification of potential hazards

         Onsite inspections

 

GENERGY's IRI will help you:

         Prevents electrical fires

         Eliminates the need for costly repairs
