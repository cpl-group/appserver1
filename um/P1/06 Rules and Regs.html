Rules and Regs.

 

  1. Overtime will be paid only if pre approved.

 

2.        Job folders are to be maintained by the project manager until the job is completed and 100% billed. Thereafter, folders are to be filed in number sequence.

 

  1. Positive attitude and teamwork are a must.  The management will not tolerate offensive language, offensive gestures, or gossip of any sort whether general or aimed at other employees, present or not.

 

  1. The office is reserved for office work during normal bussiness hours.  Leave your personal stuff home!

 

  1. Vacation schedules for one week or more vacation time are to be submitted 45 days in advance.  Personal time should be requested 48 hours in advance whenever possible. 

 

  1. Each employee is responsible for maintaining a neat and ordered work station.  Department leaders are responsible for the overall appearance of their respective work areas.

 

  1. Each department leader will assign lunch periods so that personnel is present at all times.

 

  1. All correspondence, proposals and purchase orders are to be approved by the respective department leader and/or upper management.

 

  1. Time sheets are a testament of work performed.  The employees signature is proof of that testament.  Time sheets are to be filled out daily, no exceptions.

 

  1. Employees are to use Microsoft Outlook as their calendar and event scheduler.
